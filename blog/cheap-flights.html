How to Book Cheap Flights from Nigeria

üìÖ Published: February 1, 2026 ‚è±Ô∏è 5 min read ‚úçÔ∏è By Cora Travel Team

Introduction

Traveling internationally from Nigeria doesn't have to break the bank. With the right strategies and insider knowledge, you can find amazing flight deals that save you hundreds of thousands of naira. In this comprehensive guide, we'll share proven tips and tricks from our travel experts at Cora Global Solutions.

1. Master the Booking Window

Timing is everything when booking flights. Research shows that the optimal time to book international flights is:

  • International flights: 2-3 months in advance
  • Domestic flights: 1-2 months ahead
  • Peak travel periods: Book even earlier (3-4 months)
üí° Pro Tip: Tuesday and Wednesday departures are typically cheaper than Friday-Sunday flights. Book your flights on Tuesdays or Wednesdays for maximum savings.

2. Use Flight Comparison Tools

Don't rely on a single booking platform. Compare prices across multiple sites:

  • Google Flights - Excellent for price tracking
  • Skyscanner - Wide range of airlines
  • Kayak - Good for comparing hotel + flight deals
  • Airline websites directly - Sometimes hidden discounts
‚úàÔ∏è Insider Tip: Enable price alerts on Google Flights and Skyscanner for your desired routes. Get notified immediately when prices drop!

3. Consider Flexible Dates

Your flexibility can translate directly into savings:

  • Prices can vary by $100+ between consecutive days
  • Mid-week flights are usually cheaper
  • Red-eye flights (late night) often have discounts
  • Check multiple date combinations before deciding

4. Leverage Seasonal Advantages

Flight prices follow predictable seasonal patterns:

Season Price Level Best Time to Book
Low Season (May-Aug) üü¢ Cheapest 1-2 months before
Shoulder Season üü° Moderate 2-3 months before
Peak Season (Dec-Jan) üî¥ Most Expensive 3+ months before

5. Try Alternative Routes

Flying direct isn't always the cheapest. Consider:

  • Connecting flights through hubs (Addis Ababa, Casablanca, etc.)
  • Flying into alternative airports (slightly further from your destination)
  • Multi-city itineraries instead of round-trip
  • Using budget airlines when available

6. Take Advantage of Airline Deals

  • Join frequent flyer programs for price drops
  • Follow airlines on social media for flash sales
  • Subscribe to airline newsletters
  • Check error fares on travel blogs

7. Use Incognito/Private Mode

üîí Privacy Tip: Websites track your searches using cookies. Always use incognito mode when searching for flights to avoid price increases based on your search history.

8. Clear Your Browser Cookies

Some booking sites increase prices for returning visitors. Clear your cookies between searches or use:

  • Incognito/Private browsing mode
  • Different browsers
  • VPN services (varies by location pricing)

Conclusion

Booking cheap flights from Nigeria requires a combination of strategy, timing, and flexibility. By implementing these tips, you can expect to save 20-40% compared to last-minute bookings.
